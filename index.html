The Artificial Human Eye in Space

Known as the grandaddy of modern telescopes, Sapce Telescope Hubble has changed the way astronomers see this emptiness we call space. Named after American astronomer Edwin Powel Hubble.

Dr. Hubble confirmed an "expanding" universe, which provided the foundation for the Big Bang theory. Launched in April 24, 1990, from space shuttle Discovery (STS-31), it has provided stunning images that even astronomers were shocked when they saw them for the first time.
